Staff

The Belmont Community Center is run by a well-trained staff that loves working with the children of the community. In addition to its regular staff, the center is assisted by various local college students throughout the year.

Board Members

The Belmont Community Center Board is made up of volunteer parents who meet once a month on the last Tuesday of the month. The Community Center greatly appreciates the service the members provide for their community and welcome any parents that would be interested in helping.

Anyone interested in becoming a volunteer board member or learning more about the board should contact Emily Koopmann at 402-477-8854.
